This site is complementary to Android VolleyScoreKeeper. It was thought to be able to see and follow in real time the volleyball matches that application users are watching.

What is Volley ScoreKeeper

It is an Android application that allows you to keep track of volleyball matches you're watching.
In real time you can see the players, as they are placed, you can view the results of sets played, you can share the results of the match ended on social networks to show them to your friends or to people who follow your favorite team.
You can also save your game just completed to look at the data later.
And finally you can broadcast the match live, while you're looking at, to allow even those at home to follow it step by step.
